How

Step One:

Read today’s schedule scripture passage and let one verse find you. The verse that reveals itself to your life, that verse that draws you in, challenges you, speaks directly to you. Write that verse down.

Scripture

Step Two:

Then take some time to think about that one verse. Think how it applied when it was written. Now think how it applies to you. What’s the Holy Spirit saying to you?

Observation

Step Three:

Reflect, ponder, consider, think about it, quiet your mind, and then write down your thoughts on how this insight applies to you.

Application

Step Four:

Respond to step 2 and 3 with authentic communication, write down your your prayer or your point of thanksgiving.

Prayer
